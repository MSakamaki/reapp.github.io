Announcing Reapp Beta and Reapp UI 0.11

Today I'm incredibly proud to announce the exit of alpha for Reapp UI, along with the 0.11 release of reapp-ui.

We've come an incredibly long way from the announcement two months ago. In that short time, our in house team has built three app store applications. We've integrated with Cordova, and then integrated that with Amazon Login, Firebase, and a whole host of other endpoints.

We've added lots of new components:

  • Gallery
  • Chat
  • Swiper (Swipe for options on lists)
  • Drawer ViewLists

If you're curious as to more of what's new since launch, here's the full changelog:
